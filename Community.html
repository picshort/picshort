Community Guidelines

Summary

We desire Picshort to be an authentic and safe place for inspiration and expression. Take care of this community and maintain the positivity. Follow the guidelines and laws. Do not spread hatred and negativity. Respect everyone and don’t post something illegal, nudity, or any provoking post. Post only your content. Your usage of our Platform is Picshort Content and Community Guidelines. If any of our users report your content to be against the Picshort Content Community Guidelines, we may remove such content from our Platform. If multiple reports are made regarding violation of the Picshort Content and Community Guidelines, we may be compelled to terminate your account with us and block you from registering with us. If you wish to appeal any such removal, you may write to us at info@picshort.com

Guidelines

Picshort has open community with vast diversity community of cultures, ages, and beliefs. Our team worked hard to provide this platform to create beautiful environment for everyone. These guidelines is made to foster and protect the community. By using our Picshort, you agree to follow all the guidelines and our Terms and Conditions. We follow these guidelines strictly and we expect the same from you. Not following the guidelines may result in deleted content, disabled accounts, or other restrictions.
Sometimes, the content for public awareness is allowed which would go against our community guidelines- if it is newsworthy and in the public cases. This is done for only few cases only. We do this only after weighing the public interest value against the risk of harm and we look to international human rights standards to make these judgments.

  • Share the content which you own or have the right to share.

    • Always share the content which you have taken or own it. The content which you post on Picshort should be authentic, and should be your own. Don’t try to post anything that you do not own or have any right to share.

  • Post the right content

    • We understand sometimes people wants to post the content which has nude images that are artistic or creative in nature, but the platform is operated by many individuals and for various reasons we don’t allow nudity on Picshort. This includes photos, videos, and some digitally-created content that show sexual intercourse, genitals, and close-ups of fully-nude buttocks. It also includes some photos of female nipples, but photos of post-mastectomy scarring and women actively breastfeeding are allowed.
    For safety reasons, if you post any nude images, explicit content, partially-nude children, porn, images or videos we shall remove you content from our platform. Even when this content is shared with good intentions, it could be used by others in unanticipated ways.

  • Support and encourage good and genuine interactions

    • Nobody like repetitive actions for the same things. Don’t spam the platform by providing artificially collecting likes, followers, or shares, posting repetitive comments or content, or repeatedly contacting people for commercial purposes without their consent. We do not ask money for likes, comments, share or to open your account on our platform. Don’t entertain by giving money to anyone. Don’t mislead or give fake information regarding the likes, ratings or comments.
    You can keep your username as per your choice but don’t try to impersonate others or steal anyone’s else identity. Don’t create account with others name just to mislead anyone. According to Section 66C person it is illegal to steal someone else identity and using it for misleading.

  • Follow the law.

    • Picsart is a platform which expect to express positivity, happiness, ideas and love to the community. Do not support or promote any kind of terrorism, organized crime, or hate groups. Do not perform any illegal work, illegal trade on the platform.  Offering sexual services, buying or selling firearms, alcohol, and tobacco products between private individuals, and buying or selling illegal or prescription drugs (even if legal in your region) are also not allowed. You are also not allowed to buy or sell animals or coordinate poaching.
    Sharing of child porn, organizing any kind of illegal work, buying and selling of illegal items are strictly prohibited. We sincerely do not allow these types of activities and have zero tolerance towards it.

  • Respect everyone on the Picshort community.

    • Respect every member of the Picshort community. We expect you to foster happy and positive community. Don’t do cyber bullying, hatred messages, hate comments, mental torture by repeatedly threatening the user on their profile. Do not encourage hate speech, shame anyone, harass or blackmail anyone for your personal benefits.
    It's never OK to encourage violence or attack anyone based on their race, ethnicity, national origin, sex, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, religious affiliation, disabilities, or diseases. Do no perform any financial fraud or thug anyone. Respect each and every one and grow together.

  • Be thoughtful when posting newsworthy events.

    • Do not post any content which contains sharp intense violence. We understand some people share news and information to raise awareness but do not try to spread hatred news, fake news or any disturbing type of videos which may influence people to take wrong actions or provoke them to take wrong actions.
    Do not encourage or urge people to do self-harm activity. Do not disrespect any religion, cast or sex. Do not post something hatred or provoking against any other community.
    Maintain this community positivity and foster the supportive and beautiful environment on Picshort Platform.
